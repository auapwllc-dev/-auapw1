Kia History

Kia — Complete Brand History

Kia Corporation was founded in 1944 manufacturing steel tubing and bicycle parts. Acquired by Hyundai in 1998 after bankruptcy. Designer Peter Schreyer (former Audi designer of TT) transformed Kia's design in 2006. The Telluride SUV won Car of the Year multiple times. Kia offers America's best warranty at 10 years/100,000 miles powertrain.

Kia Parts FAQ

Where do AUAPW.ORG's Kia parts come from?
All Kia parts are sourced from our network of 2,000+ verified salvage yards and auto recyclers across all 50 US states. Every yard is vetted for quality standards, accurate part grading, and customer service before joining our network. When you submit a request, our system simultaneously queries the entire network to find the best match for your specific Kia model, year, and trim level.
How long does it take to get a Kia part?
After you submit a quote request, you'll receive pricing and availability within 24 hours — guaranteed. Once you confirm your order, most Kia parts ship within 1-3 business days. Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days to anywhere in the contiguous United States. Larger items like engines and transmissions may require freight shipping with 5-10 day delivery.
What warranty comes with used Kia parts?
Every Kia part sourced through AUAPW.ORG includes a 30-180 day warranty directly from the supplying yard. Engines and transmissions typically carry 90-180 day warranties. Electrical components and body parts carry 30-90 day warranties. The warranty covers the part performing as described — if there's an issue, we'll arrange a replacement or full refund.
Can AUAPW.ORG source Kia parts that are no longer in production?
Yes! Our network specializes in hard-to-find parts for older and discontinued Kia models. Because we're connected to 2,000+ salvage yards nationwide, we have access to a vast inventory of parts for vehicles going back to the early 1990s. Contact us with your specific Kia part need and we'll do our best to locate it.
