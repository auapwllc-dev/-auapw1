Hyundai History

Hyundai — Complete Brand History

Hyundai Motor Company was founded on December 29, 1967, by Chung Ju-yung in Seoul, South Korea. The company's first venture was building licensed Ford Cortinas in 1968. Hyundai developed its first independently engineered car, the Pony, in 1975 with design help from Giorgetto Giugiaro (who also designed the VW Golf) and powertrain by Mitsubishi. The Pony became South Korea's first domestically developed car. Hyundai entered the American market in 1986 with the Excel, becoming the most successful first-year launch by any import brand in US history, selling 168,882 cars. However, quality issues plagued early Hyundais and the brand spent years rebuilding its reputation. Chung Mong-koo took over in 1998 and implemented a quality revolution, hiring away executives from major European automakers and offering America's best warranty: 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain. By 2008, Consumer Reports was recommending Hyundai vehicles. The Genesis luxury division was spun off as a separate brand in 2015. The Elantra, Sonata, Santa Fe, and Tucson have become genuine competitors to Japanese rivals. The Ioniq 5 and Ioniq 6 electric vehicles have won numerous global awards. Hyundai's Theta II GDI engines had a significant recall for oil starvation issues in 2015-2018 production, which is an important consideration when sourcing used engines.

Hyundai Parts FAQ

What is the Hyundai Theta II engine recall about?
Hyundai and Kia issued major recalls for Theta II GDI 2.0L and 2.4L engines produced 2011-2019 due to manufacturing debris causing oil starvation and engine seizure/fire risk. AUAPW.ORG carefully screens used Theta II engines and can provide information about recall status on specific VINs.
Are Hyundai transmissions reliable?
Hyundai's 6-speed automatic (6T45/6T30) used in Sonata and Elantra is generally reliable. The DCT dual-clutch transmission in some models had known issues. The newer 8DCT and iMT (intelligent Manual Transmission) have improved. AUAPW.ORG sources tested units for all Hyundai models.
